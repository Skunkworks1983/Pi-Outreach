Instructions:

1 Copy entire Client folder onto computer
2 run DriverStation.jar while making sure required dll's are present in folder that driverstation exists

Once inside application
1 hold down a or b button on one controller, click register controller on desired robot color
2 move joysticks to make sure controller registration was successful
3 click connect and the robot should move now
4 repeate steps 1-3 for the remaining three robots

Do not close program by ending the process or other method than clicking the red X at the 
top right of the application, otherwise the socket will not be closed properly
